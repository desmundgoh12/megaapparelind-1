About us

Established in 2009, Mega Apparel Industries is an end-to-end custom garment and OEM manufacturer dedicated to providing reliable, high-quality apparel solutions. For nearly two decades, we have partnered with businesses, independent brands, schools, and corporations to deliver tailored garment production built on a foundation of honesty and consistency.

We specialize in business-to-business custom manufacturing, combining skilled workmanship with a deep commitment to provide quality. While we leverage modern manufacturing standards, we believe our true strength lies in the trust, attention to detail, and responsibility we bring to every order.

At Mega Apparel Industries, we don't just manufacture garments—we build long-term partnerships focused on quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

How can we help you

Low MOQ

We accept a minimum order of 50-100 units (or even less).

OEM & Custom Made

From unique fabric selections and custom sizing to precise design specifications, we provide fully customized, tailor-made garment manufacturing.

One Stop Manufacturing

We specialize in:
  • Silkscreen Printing
  • Heat Transfer
  • Embroidery
  • Sublimation
  • High Density

Consistent Quality

With nearly 20 years of experience, we carefully manage every step of production, from the first stitch to the final quality check. Using quality materials and attention to detail, we ensure every order is made to meet your standards.

On-Time Delivery

Each order is carefully managed to meet agreed deadlines, ensuring a smooth and dependable production process from start to finish.
